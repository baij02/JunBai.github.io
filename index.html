    About me

    I am a Ph.d. candidate from University of Connecticut Computer Sicence and Engineering Department. I work for Dr. Sheida Nabavi's lab. In the past few years, I spend my time to develop deep learning models to analysis medical images includes X-ray images, Computed Tomosynthesis (CT), and cell images.

    The tasks of my research broad to classification, segmentation, synthesising, and reconstructing medical images. My research contributed to diagnostic breast cancer, predict COVID-19 ICU demanding, and reconstructing cell objects.

    In the past few years, I published six journal articals and two conference full papers.
